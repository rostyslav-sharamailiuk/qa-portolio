**Title:** Filter button opens empty modal in Brand category

**Environment:**
- Browser: Safari 13.4
- OS: Apple iOS
- Device: Apple Iphone 11 Pro Max

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open the side bar and choose any of 3 product categories
2. Choose one of the two brand options
3. Click on  "Filter" button
4. Refresh the page

**Expected Result:**
Filters are shown or there are is at least a way to close a modal

**Actual Result:**
Empty modal is shown and user can't close it and has to manually navigate back to "Brands" page

**Severity:** Medium  
**Priority:** Medium
